How to Play

Before you begin, you should be aware that Valorant has 13 rounds and one life per player every round. You utilize your powers and weapons to go against opponents, and as an attacker, you try to plant the spike, while as a defender, you strive to prevent the other team from planting the spike

Agent selection

Before the game starts, you get to pick an agent, each with their own abilities and roles.

Sides

You begin the game on either the offensive or defensive side, which is switched mid-game. Remember that in order to begin the round, you must first purchase a weapon from the shop. What you buy determines whether or not you win a round.

Map

Collaborate with your team to attack the map and find your best bet for either planting or defusing the spike. Keep an eye on your angles, coordinate collectively, and adapt together during the game.

Tips

To properly utilize and be the best player, be sure to play wisely by listening for enemies, stopping before shooting, and utilizing a combination of abilities and weapons.
