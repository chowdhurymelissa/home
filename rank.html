Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Ascendant, Immortal, and Radiant are the ranks. Each rank, with the exception of Radiant, has three levels. The greater the number, the higher your ranking. Over time, your rank will have a direct impact on your Act Rank.
